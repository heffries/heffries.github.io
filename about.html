About Me

HI! I AM JEFFREY!

Hi my name is Jeffrey and I am based in NYC. My style of shooting is mainly portraits, lifestyle, and street photography.

I am currently shooting with a Sony a7ii with a wide and portrait lens with an off camera flash.

A little background about me:

I have always had a passion for photography but never had the fund to purchase a camera. After working a few years in finance in the corporate world and doing weekend DJ gigs, I was able to purchase my first mirrorless camera. I got my first camera around 2015 St. Patrick's Day and have been practicing since then. I have tried lots of different style of photography but Portraits, Street, and Lifestyle really stuck with me. I worked at Foot Locker, INC. for about 4 years and had grown a huge passion for streetwear, which also got me into Portraits and Lifestyle photography.
